FAQs

Q1: What is WECE Hacks?

A: WECE Hacks is an annual hackathon organized by the WECE (Women in Electrical and Computer Engineering) club, where students come together to create innovative technology.

Q2: How do I sign up?

A: You can register for WECE Hacks by visiting the registration page on our website. Make sure to create your team and prepare for the event!

Q3: What are the rules of the event?

A: The rules can be found in the FAQ section or in the event guidelines document. Be sure to review them before participating!

Q4: Will food be provided at WECE Hacks 2025?

A: Full meals, snacks, and drinks will be provided and FREE!
